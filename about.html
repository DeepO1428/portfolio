About Me

Hi, I’m Amandeep Kaur, a passionate Web and UI/UX Designer with over 4 years of experience in crafting functional, visually appealing, and user-centered digital experiences. My professional journey began in India, where I earned a Master’s degree in Information Technology (MSc IT) and worked as a Web Designer. During this time, I specialized in designing responsive websites, creating interactive layouts, and delivering seamless user experiences.
After moving to Canada, I pursued a post-graduate diploma in Computer Applications Development, which helped me deepen my technical expertise in web development and programming. Currently, I’m advancing my skills further by studying Interaction Media Management, where I focus on integrating cutting-edge technology with innovative design strategies to create meaningful digital solutions.
Beyond work, I find inspiration in cooking, exploring diverse cultures, and playing sports, which fuel my creativity and help me stay grounded. Let’s connect and bring your vision to life!
