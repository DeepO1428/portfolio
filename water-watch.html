Water Watch

An innovative app that helps users locate safe drinking water globally. This project focused on creating an intuitive interface with real-time data to ensure a smooth user experience for travelers and locals alike.
Year 2024

Project Overview

Goal: To provide an intuitive platform for finding clean drinking water globally.

My Role: UI/UX Designer

Tools Used: Figma, Zoom, Research Methods

Process: The project involved user research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. I work collabratively on each part.

Key Features

  • Real-time water quality data from verified sources
  • User-friendly map interface for locating water stations
  • Crowdsourced feedback and water quality rankings
  • Notifications and alerts for water resources
